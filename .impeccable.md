## Design Context

### Users
Indian crypto investors during tax season (January–March). This is a calm, deliberate planning session, usually done on a laptop, intended for making one-time tax decisions. A secondary mobile context exists for quick eligibility checks.

### Brand Personality
- **Authoritative**: Credible, serious, and professional. Feels like a financial instrument or a CA-built tool.
- **Precise**: Numerical specificity is prioritized. No aggressive rounding. Structured and aligned data.
- **Understated**: Minimal noise, no gradients or illustrations. Lets the data speak. Muted corporate palette.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Lead with **Dark Mode**. Reflects a professional terminal feel.
- **Visual Tone**: Data-dense technical but with significant breathing room (whitespace). More like a refined brokerage statement than a hectic trading terminal.
- **Details**: 
    - Monospaced or tabular-figure numbers for alignment.
    - Subtle grid lines in tables, no heavy borders.
    - Soft, purposeful entrances (fades/staggers).
    - Muted secondary text hierarchy.
    - Savings message is a quiet reward, not a loud banner.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity First**: Jargon-heavy concepts must be instantly understandable through layout and labeling.
2. **Authoritative Trust**: Use structured data and precise numbers to build confidence in the calculations.
3. **Effortless Comparison**: The Pre vs. After harvesting comparison must be the center of gravity.
4. **Contextual Familiarity**: Use Indian currency formatting (lakhs/crores) to signal local expertise.
5. **Intentionality over Intensity**: Choose bold directions (like deep dark themes) but execute them with surgical precision and restraint.
